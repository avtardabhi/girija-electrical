Our Safety Commitment

Engineering Safer Electrical Infrastructure Before Incidents Occur

Every Electrical Fire Has a Cause. Every Cause Can Be Identified. Every Incident Can Be Prevented.

The Reality

Across India, electrical faults contribute to many fire incidents in residential, commercial and industrial buildings. Hidden deficiencies such as overloaded circuits, loose connections, deteriorated insulation, inadequate protection systems and poor maintenance often remain unnoticed until an incident occurs. At GIRIJA, we believe these risks should be identified before they become emergencies.

Our Philosophy

We don't wait for electrical failures—we work to prevent them. Every inspection, measurement and engineering recommendation is focused on creating safer electrical infrastructure.

Our Engineering Approach

UNDERSTAND

Understand the system, site conditions and operation requirements.

INSPECT

Thorough on-site inspection of electrical infrastructure and systems.

EVALUATE

Engineering evaluation to identify risks and non-compliances.

RECOMMEND

Practical and prioritized recommendations for corrective actions.

IMPROVE

Support implementation for safer and more reliable electrical infrastructure.

Scope of Electrical Safety Audit

HT & LT Systems

Inspection of electrical distribution systems for safety, reliability, and compliance.

Transformers & RMU

Assessment of transformer and RMU performance, protection, and operational condition.

DG, UPS & Inverters

Evaluation of backup power systems for uninterrupted and safe operation.

Electrical Panels

Inspection of switchboards and panels for faults, overheating and maintenance.

Earthing & Grounding

Verification of grounding systems for effective fault protection and safety.

Lightning Protection

Inspection of lightning protection and surge protection systems.

Cable Trays & Routing

Assessment of cable routing, support systems and installation practices.

Electrical Rooms

Review of electrical room safety, ventilation, housekeeping and accessibility.

Protection Coordination

Analysis of protective device settings for reliable fault isolation.

Electrical Fire Risk Assessment

Identification of potential electrical fire hazards and preventive measures.
