Rahulkumar Padhiar

Founder & Principal Electrical Consultant

"Engineering is not just about powering buildings— it is about protecting lives, safeguarding property."

Rahulkumar Padhiyar founded GIRIJA Electricals & Engineering Services with a commitment to raising the standard of electrical engineering consultancy in Surat, Gujarat. Early in his career, he recognized that while electrical systems are among the most critical elements of any building, they are often overlooked during the planning and design stages. This realization inspired him to establish a consultancy dedicated to delivering thoughtfully engineered, safe and future-ready electrical infrastructure rather than treating electrical design as merely a compliance requirement.

His work combines technical precision with practical engineering, helping architects, developers and industries create electrical systems that are efficient, scalable, reliable and inherently safe. With expertise in electrical infrastructure planning, power distribution, lighting engineering, earthing systems, lightning protection, ELV coordination and electrical safety audits, he believes every engineering decision should contribute to protecting lives, enhancing reliability and creating long-term value.

Driven by the mission of "Making Infrastructure Electrically Safe," he is committed to promoting a culture of proactive electrical engineering where safety is considered from the very first design decision—not after construction is completed.

His long-term vision is clear:

To make infrastructure electrically safe and contribute towards an Electrical Fire Free Nation.
