About Company

Who We Are

At GIRIJA Electricals & Engineering Consultants, we design intelligent engineering solutions that protect lives, safeguard property and reduce electrical fire risks through safe, reliable and code-compliant electrical infrastructure.

GIRIJA Electricals & Engineering Consultants is a Surat-based engineering consultancy delivering Electrical Design and MEP Engineering solutions for residential, commercial, industrial, institutional and infrastructure projects. Every design reflects our commitment to safety, reliability and long-term performance.
